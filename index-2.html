PIXELSPACE

Collaborative Game Canvas and Editable Background

WHAT IS PIXELSPACE?

PixelSpace is the collaborative canvas space background for AYYO games.

Select the Paint Brush

Choose Your Colors

Showcase Your Pixels

DRAW WITH FRIENDS

Pick up the Paint Brush and begin coloring in the game as you play. Players can draw temporary graffiti anywhere on the game map. Sharing doodles, secret messages, or hand drawn rocket ships!

AUCTION AND BID
ON PIXELS

PixelSpace allows anyone to paint temporary graffiti without having to purchase pixels. If youâ€™d like your paint to last permanently you can bid for the rights to paint the area at the Auction House. These pixels can later be put up for sale at the auction house.

SHOWCASE YOUR
PIXEL ART

Want your Pixel Art in the game? Purchase an area of pixels ( 64x64 for example ) and paint a custom image on your reserved pixels. Great for memes, advertisements, or general tom-foolery.
